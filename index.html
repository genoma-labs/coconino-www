The studio

We named it after the dog.

He was manifested in the Coconino National Forest. Some time later and a long way from those pines, a mother doberman had a litter — and one of those puppies turned out to be him.

He has floppy ears, two tan eyebrow dots, and firm opinions about whether anyone should still be working this late. He supervises. He is on everything we make, down in the corner, watching.

Manifested in the Coconino National Forest. Delivered, some months later, by a doberman.
